Power Notepad



Downlad App



Power notepad is a notepad better than your systems default notepad. It is packed with more features, a more Beautiful, playful, unique GUI and comes with a rich and powerful experience.



Many features coming soon

  • partialy custom themes
  • font sizes, custom fonts
  • markdown support
  • rtf support
  • pdf viewing
  • word document viewing
  • html editor
  • extension support
  • photo viewer
  • audio player
  • video player
  • and maybe more
Most of the feature are theoretical and won’t even pass the testing but this is the list.



The app is also very hackable making it perfect for making it your own.
